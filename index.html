Yash Mehta

PhD student, Johns Hopkins University.

I’m a PhD student in Computational Cognitive Science at Johns Hopkins University, studying representations in biological and artificial neural networks. My research explores the parallels between how brains and AI systems learn to internally represent and information. In summer 2025, I’m working at Sakana AI as a research scientist intern, working on autonomous systems for open-ended scientific discovery. I also co-founded an early-stage AI startup and previously was a software developer at Amazon.
